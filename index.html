Pizza!

Pizza (Italian: {' pittsa'}, Neapolitan: {'pittsa}) is a savory dish of Italian origin consisting of a usally round, flattened base of leavened wheat-base dough topped with tomatoes,cheese and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies,mushrooms,onions,olives,pineapple,meat, etc),which is then baked at a high temperature,traditionally in a wood-fired oven.{1} A small pizzac is sometimes called a pizzetta. A person who makes pizza is known as a pizzaiolo.
